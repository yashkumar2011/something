Change of Habits for More Focus

1. Single tasking

In this world we are often told that to be more productive we must be able to do multiples tasks at once but tbh our attention drops exponentially depending on the number of tasks we are actaully focusing on. Our brain just creates and illusion that we are multitasking but in reality we are shifting our focus from one taks to another so quickly that we our attention get so fucked up that you can't even imagine.This also includes listening to music while working or watching something on TV while eating. Make are rule from now. ONE THING AT A TIME.
